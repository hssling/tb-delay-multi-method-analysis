Manuscript Title: Precision Synthesis of Tuberculosis Detection Delays in India: A Unified Bayesian, PCA, and DAG Framework

Author: Dr. H S Siddalingaiah

Ethics Approval Status:

This study involves secondary analysis of publicly available data sources and does not require ethics committee approval as per ICMJE guidelines for studies not involving human participants or identifiable private information.

Data Sources Used:
1. World Health Organization Global Tuberculosis Report 2024
2. Central TB Division, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India - India TB Report 2024
3. National Family Health Survey (NFHS-5), 2019-2021
4. Census of India 2011
5. Published systematic literature reviews (secondary analysis)

All data sources are publicly available and do not contain individual patient identifiers. No primary data collection involving human participants was conducted.
